Arrangement for the New Academic Year 2020/2021

To all Hong Kong Institute of Vocational Education (Sha Tin) new students:

To help you adapt to the new learning environment, please note the following information regarding the arrangement of the new academic year:

The 1st Installment of Fee Payment (Only applicable to Full-time new students)
Please settle the 1st Installment of Fee Payment before 14 August 2020.If you fail to pay the required fee by the due date, you will be de-registered from the programme. All fees paid are non-refundable.

Date of Class Commencement (Tentative)
The new academic year would commence on 1 September 2020 (Tuesday) for Full-time new students and 7 September 2020 (Monday) for Part-time new students.

Student card
Student card would be issued and distributed to new students through Department during the orientation day / the class commencement week, subject to students’ completion of admission registration procedure.

Student Handbook
You may access to the “MyPortal” (https://myportal.vtc.edu.hk/wps/portal) or visit the campus intranet of IVE(ST) (http://stintra.vtc.edu.hk/) for contents of the Student Handbook about one week before the commencement of class.

Dissemination of Campus Information
The Campus normally makes use of the MyPortal (https://myportal.vtc.edu.hk/wps/portal) and emails as communication channels with students. To obtain updated news of the Campus, please visit the MyPortal and check your student email account regularly.

New Student Information Website
New Student can also visit the website (http://stsdo.vtc.edu.hk/admission/) which including:

  • Orientation Day (Only applicable to Full-time new students): The information about the Orientation Day will be uploaded at New Student Information website in mid-August.

  • Timetable: You may access to the “MyPortal” (https://myportal.vtc.edu.hk/wps/portal) for details of the timetable about one week before the commencement of class. Part-time new students may also visit the website of IVE(ST) (http://st.vtc.edu.hk/esu/Timetable-c.htm).

  • Application for Module Exemption / Credit Transfer: Application for module exemption / credit transfer for AY2020/2021should be submittedby the end of Week Two of the first academic year / first semester (for new students). Application forms will be available at Room 023 One Stop Student Services Centre.

  • Application for Financial Assistance Schemes:
    Full-time Programmes: For studying Higher Diploma Programme students, you can apply for financial assistance offered by the government’s Student Finance Office (SFO). And for studying Diploma of Foundation Studies students, you can applyforthe fee remission scheme offered by VTC through Student Development Office(SDO). For details, please visit http://stsdo.vtc.edu.hk/url/sfaa.pdf.

    Part-time Programmes: Students can apply for Extended Non-means-tested Loan Scheme (ENLS) by the government’s Student Finance Office. Some programmes joined the Vplus Engineering Subsidy Scheme(Enquiry: 2435 9423). Students should be aware of the eligibility of financial assistance scheme and subsidy scheme. In respect of any courses, modules or units of study credits, students may not be able to apply for more than one financial assistance scheme or subsidy scheme.
